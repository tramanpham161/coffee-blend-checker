**Coffee Blend Checker**

_Commercial & Sustainability Decision Support Tool for UK FMCG Procurement_

A high-precision analytical tool designed to help Coffee Category Buyers and Procurement Managers navigate the "triple squeeze" of the 2026 coffee market: Arabica price volatility, GBP/USD exchange rate fluctuations, and mandatory ESG (Carbon) reporting.

**The Problem**

The UK coffee industry is currently facing record-high Arabica prices and a volatile Sterling. Traditional procurement often relies on static spreadsheets that fail to show the real-time impact of blend changes on both the bottom line (Profit) and the planet (Carbon).

**The Solution**

This tool allows procurement professionals to:
- **Stress-test Margins**: Instantly see how a 5-10% drop in the Pound (£) affects the cost of a 250g retail bag.
- **Optimise Blends**: Simulate the transition from Arabica to high-quality Vietnamese Robusta to recover gross margins.
- **Quantify ESG**: Use Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) data to prove the carbon reduction of different sourcing strategies.
- **Scenario Planning**: Save and export multiple "What-If" scenarios to Excel for stakeholder presentations.

**Key Features**

- **Dynamic FX Engine**: Manual exchange rate inputs to model currency sensitivity.
- **Before vs. After Analysis**: Side-by-side comparison of current vs. proposed sourcing strategies.
- **Carbon Intelligence**: Integrated CO2e tracking based on IDH Sustainable Trade data (Arabica 1.2kg vs. Robusta 0.4kg per kg).
- **Scenario Tracker**: A session-state history table that allows for multi-strategy auditing and CSV export.
- **Minimalist UI**: Designed for executive decision-making with a clean, British-focused aesthetic.

**Sourcing Logic & Data**

- **Landed Cost Calculation**: Includes raw material costs converted from USD to GBP, adjusted for a standard 250g retail pack.
- **Carbon Footprint**: Data based on _IDH Sustainable Trade & Cirad Life-Cycle Assessments_.
- **Margin Targets**: Visual indicators flag when a blend falls below standard UK retail margin requirements.

**Why this matters for your business**

By 2026, transparency in both cost-to-serve and carbon-to-serve is a competitive requirement. This tool provides the "Commercial Intelligence" needed to maintain shelf-presence and profitability in a disrupted global market.
